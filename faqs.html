Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your tour packages?

Our packages include transportation, accommodation, meals, and tour guides, depending on the package you choose.

Do I need to make a deposit to book a tour?

Yes, we require a 30% deposit to secure your booking, and the rest is payable before the tour date.

Can I customize my tour package?

Absolutely! We offer customized tours to suit your preferences. Feel free to reach out to discuss your ideal itinerary.

What happens if I cancel my booking?

We have a cancellation policy. Depending on how much time before the tour date, you may receive a full or partial refund.
